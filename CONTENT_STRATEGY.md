## SEO Keyword Research

### Understanding Keyword Intent

**Types of Search Intent:**

1. **Informational:** "what is ChatGPT"
   - User wants to learn
   - Low conversion
   - Good for traffic

2. **Commercial:** "best AI writing tools"
   - User comparing options
   - Medium conversion
   - Good for affiliate revenue

3. **Transactional:** "Jasper discount code"
   - User ready to buy
   - High conversion
   - Best for revenue

**Focus:** 70% commercial, 20% informational, 10% transactional

### Keyword Research Process

#### Step 1: Seed Keywords

**Core Topics:**
- AI writing tools
- AI image generators
- AI video tools
- AI chatbots
- AI coding assistants
- AI productivity tools

#### Step 2: Use Keyword Research Tools

**Free Tools:**

1. **Google Autocomplete:**
```
Type: "best AI" → See suggestions
- best AI writing tools
- best AI image generator
- best AI chatbot
- best AI for coding
```

2. **People Also Ask:**
- Google shows related questions
- Each is a potential article

3. **Related Searches:**
- Bottom of Google results
- Shows related queries

**Paid Tools (Optional):**

1. **Ahrefs Keyword Explorer:**
   - Monthly search volume

   - Cost: $99/month

   - Similar to Ahrefs
   - Content gap analysis

   - Ubersuggest: $29/month

#### Step 3: Analyze Keyword Metrics

**Key Metrics:**

| Keyword | Volume | Difficulty | CPC | Priority |
|---------|--------|------------|-----|----------|
| AI writing tools | 14,800 | 65 | $5.20 | High |
| Jasper AI review | 2,400 | 45 | $8.30 | High |
| Copy.ai vs Jasper | 720 | 35 | $6.50 | Medium |

- Volume: 500-5,000/month

### Keyword Difficulty Guide

**0-20 (Easy):**
- Can rank with basic content
- Target these first
- Example: "Leonardo AI review 2026"

**20-40 (Medium):**
- Need quality content + some backlinks
- Target after 3-6 months
- Example: "best AI image generators"

**40-60 (Hard):**
- Need excellent content + many backlinks
- Target after 6-12 months
- Example: "AI writing tools"

**60-100 (Very Hard):**
- Extremely competitive
- Need authority site + lots of backlinks
- Consider long-tail variations instead
- Example: "ChatGPT"

## Content Templates

### Template 1: Tool Review

**Target Keyword:** "[Tool Name] Review 2026"

**Structure:**

```markdown
# [Tool Name] Review 2026: Is It Worth It?

## Introduction (100-150 words)
- Brief tool overview
- Who it's for
- Key features teaser
- Price range

## What is [Tool Name]? (150-200 words)
- Detailed description
- Company background
- Target audience
- Main use cases

## Key Features (300-400 words)
### Feature 1: [Name]
- Description
- Screenshot
- Why it matters

### Feature 2: [Name]
- Description
- Screenshot
- Why it matters

(3-5 features total)

## Pricing (150-200 words)
- Free plan details
- Paid plan tiers
- Value for money
- Comparison to competitors

## Pros and Cons (200-250 words)
### Pros ✓
- Pro 1 with explanation
- Pro 2 with explanation
- Pro 3 with explanation

### Cons ✗
- Con 1 with explanation
- Con 2 with explanation
- Con 3 with explanation

## Who Should Use [Tool Name]? (150-200 words)
### Perfect For:
- Use case 1
- Use case 2

### Not Ideal For:
- Limitation 1
- Limitation 2

## [Tool Name] vs Alternatives (200-300 words)
- Brief comparison to 2-3 competitors
- Link to detailed comparisons
- Unique selling points

## Final Verdict (100-150 words)
- Summary
- Rating: X/5 stars
- Recommendation
- CTA with affiliate link

## FAQ (200-300 words)
- 5-7 common questions
- Brief answers
- Schema markup for rich snippets

---

**Total Word Count:** 1,800-2,200 words
**Target:** 2,000 words

**SEO Optimization:**
- H1: Exact target keyword
- H2s: Variations (pricing, features, pros and cons)
- Internal links: 3-5 to related tools
- External links: 1-2 to authoritative sources
- Images: 5-8 (screenshots, logo, infographic)
- Alt text: Descriptive with keyword
```

## Evergreen vs Trending Content

### Evergreen Content (70%)

**Definition:** Content that stays relevant over time

**Examples:**
- "Best AI Writing Tools" (updates annually)
- "How to Use AI for Content Marketing"
- "Jasper AI Review" (update quarterly)

**Strategy:**
- Target high-volume keywords
- Update regularly (quarterly)
- Build backlinks over time
- Compound traffic growth

**Update Schedule:**
- January: Update year in titles (2025 → 2026)
- Quarterly: Refresh data, screenshots, pricing
- As needed: When tools change significantly

### Trending Content (30%)

**Definition:** Time-sensitive, high-interest topics

**Examples:**
- "New ChatGPT Features 2026"
- "Google Gemini vs GPT-4.5"
- "Top AI Tools Launched This Month"

**Strategy:**
- Move fast (publish same day/week)
- Capture early traffic spike
- Convert to evergreen later
- Expect traffic to decay

**How to Find Trends:**

1. **Google Trends:**
   - Monitor "AI" related queries
   - Spot rising trends
   - Compare keywords

2. **Twitter:**
   - Follow #AI hashtag
   - Track tool launches
   - Spot viral moments

3. **Reddit:**
   - r/artificial
   - r/ChatGPT
   - r/singularity

4. **Product Hunt:**
   - Daily AI product launches
   - Trending AI tools
   - Early coverage opportunity

### Content Decay Strategy

**Month 1-3:** Fresh content, high rankings
**Month 4-6:** Rankings stabilize
**Month 7-12:** Refresh content
**Year 2+:** Major update or archive

**Signs Content Needs Update:**
- Traffic declining 50%+
- Ranking dropped 5+ positions
- Information outdated
- Better content published by competitors

## Internal Linking Strategy

### Why Internal Linking Matters

**Benefits:**
1. SEO: Distributes page authority
2. UX: Helps users discover content
3. Conversions: Guides to affiliate content
4. Crawling: Helps Google index pages

### Linking Structure

**Hub and Spoke Model:**

```
Category Page (Hub)
    ↓
    ├── Tool Review 1 (Spoke)
    ├── Tool Review 2 (Spoke)
    ├── Tool Review 3 (Spoke)
    └── Comparison Post (Spoke)
         ↓
         Link to Reviews
```

**Example:**

**Hub:** /category/ai-writing-tools
- Links to: All writing tools
- Links from: Homepage, footer

**Spokes:** Individual tool pages
- Link to: Category page
- Link to: Related tools
- Link to: Comparison posts

### Internal Linking Rules

**1. Contextual Links:**
```markdown
If you're looking for alternatives, check out our
[Copy.ai review](/tools/copy-ai) or compare
[Jasper vs Copy.ai](/comparisons/jasper-vs-copyai).
```

**2. Strategic Anchor Text:**
❌ "click here"
✅ "Jasper AI review"
✅ "best AI writing tools"

**3. Link to Orphan Pages:**

Find pages with no internal links:
```sql
-- Query to find orphan tools (conceptual)
SELECT slug, name
FROM tools
WHERE id NOT IN (
  SELECT DISTINCT tool_id FROM internal_links
);
```

**4. Update Old Posts:**
- When publishing new content
- Add links from relevant old posts
- Example: New tool published → Update "Best Tools" list

### Link Quantity

**Per Post:**
- 3-5 internal links minimum
- 10-15 maximum
- Natural placement
- Relevant context

**To Prioritize:**
- Money pages (affiliate reviews)
- Category pages (topical authority)
- Comparison posts (high conversion)

## Final Tips

### Content Velocity

**Phase 1 (Months 1-3):**
- Goal: Build foundation
- Target: 100-200 tools
- Manual + automated content
- Focus on quality over quantity

**Phase 2 (Months 4-6):**
- Goal: Fill gaps
- Target: 300-500 tools
- Mostly automated
- Hand-write high-value posts

**Phase 3 (Months 7+):**
- Goal: Maintain and optimize
- Target: 500+ tools
- Update existing content
- Focus on conversions

### Never Sacrifice Quality

**Good Content:**
- 2,000 words, auto-generated, basic info
- Ranks eventually
- Low engagement

**Great Content:**
- 2,500 words, hand-written, unique insights
- Ranks faster
- High engagement, backlinks, conversions

**Strategy:**
- Automated content: Volume
- Manual content: Authority
- Hybrid: Best results
